Paris Fashion Week

Paris Fashion Week (French: Semaine de la mode de Paris) is a series of designer shows that takes place every two years in Paris, France, with spring/summer and fall/winter events taking place each year. The dates are set by the French Fashion Federation. Paris Fashion Week takes place at venues all over the city. In addition to the ready-to-wear shows, there are menswear and fashion shows, held semi-annually for the spring/summer and fall/winter seasons. Also every year, famous brands such as Dior, Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Givenchy, and Celine host their shows in historical venues such as the Carrousel du Louvre and the Grand Palais.
